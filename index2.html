ABOUT DATA CABLE, INC.


Over A Quarter Century of
Professional Service,
Maintenance and Installation

Data Cable Inc. is a Union IBEW low voltage cabling contractor. We are
woman-owned and certified WBE and WOSB business through the Woman
Business Enterprise National Council (WBENC) and the SBA. Data Cable Inc. is
an industry leader in the design and installation of low voltage structured
cabling including fiber optics, closed-circuit camera systems, and more. We
strive to provide the highest level of quality and industry standards on every
service and installation project.

Our technicians are Union trained and certified, guaranteeing that our projects are up to date with the latest products and techniques. Our number one priority is
to maintain our professional level of customer service and quality workmanship. That is our promise to you.
